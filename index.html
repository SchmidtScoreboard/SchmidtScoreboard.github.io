Live Hockey Scores

Schmidt Scoreboard fetches live hockey scores, showing everything going on in the league all the time. Check when games start, track scores and powerplays. Schmidt Scoreboard can focus on your favorite team so that you never miss a moment.

Live Baseball Scores

Switch to baseball mode to catch every game. See every ball, strike, and out across the entire league. When your favorite team is playing, Schmidt Scoreboard will focus so you always stay up-to-date.

Digital Clock

Whether it's the offseason or a global pandemic and there are no games on, Scoreboard can show a digital clock. What other screens would you like to see?

Scoreboard App

The Schmidt Scoreboard App for iOS and Android lets you set up a Scoreboard, select your favorite teams, change modes, or even turn your Scoreboard off and on from anywhere in your house.

Build Your Own

Schmidt Scoreboard is fully open-source under a GNU Public License. All the code is extensible and open, so you can make your Schmidt Scoreboard do whatever you want. In depth instructions, parts lists, and STL files are coming soon!
